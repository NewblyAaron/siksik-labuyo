# Siksik Labuyo

An Android PoS & Inventory App, aimed specifically to cater the needs of the Siksik Labuyo, a small group of creators who make their own products and sell them as one banner. 

The main feature of this app is its ability to track the sales of each creatorâ€™s products. The goal is to lessen the time of tallying their products sold per creator, which is what the app is meant to do.
